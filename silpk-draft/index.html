    • Background – Why Japan?


    Japan is a rapidly aging country and is in immediate need of trained and skilled manpower. The requirement is as high as 3,00,000 engineers in the next 2 to 3 years alone. The only barrier that separates Indians from taking advantage of this opportunity is the requirement of Japanese Language Skills. Japan has come forward to offer jobs in Japan to students who are willing to learn the Japanese Language Levels - N5, N4 and N3.

    The Requirement


    Draft Japan Company Pvt. Ltd. Japan, has an immediate requirement for Engineers. Draft Japan is a leading Manpower Recruitment firm based out of Kitakyushu, Japan. SPG has been appointed as the sole recruitment partner for the Indian subcontinent by Draft Japan to identify, recruit and train technical candidates to be employed in Japan. The current requirement exceeds 200 Engineers from Computer Science, Mechanical and Electronics and Communication branches (and associated branches) of Engineering. Before a candidate is onboarded into the Job in Japan, it is mandatory for her/him to undergo training in India for a duration of 6 months. The training is imparted over two segments conducted simultaneously in India. The program shall be a full-time program. Japanese Language Training – up to Nihongo Level N3.
  • Course commencement: 16th August 2020 (Tentative)
    • Technical Training (as prescribed by Draft Japan).
  • Course commencement: 5th October 2020 (Tentative)
    • The Technical training will be coordinated by SPG and the Japanese Language Program shall be delivered by Draft Japan, Japan on an on-line mode assisted in India by SPG. The test to be attempted for the Language qualification is – JLPT/ NAT.
